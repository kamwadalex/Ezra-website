Student Life

Experience a vibrant and inclusive student life at Ezra Memorial Secondary School

Student Clubs

Debate Club

Our Debate Club provides students with opportunities to develop critical thinking, public speaking, and argumentation skills. Students participate in inter-school debates, regional competitions, and national tournaments.

  • Weekly debate sessions
  • Inter-school competitions
  • Public speaking workshops
  • Leadership development

Patriotism Club

The Patriotism Club instills love for the country and promotes national values among students. Members participate in civic education, community service, and national celebrations.

  • National anthem practice
  • Community service projects
  • Cultural heritage activities
  • Leadership training

Prefectorial Body

Student Leadership

The Prefectorial Body consists of elected student leaders who serve as role models and assist in maintaining discipline and order within the school. They represent student interests and organize various school activities.

Head Prefect

Overall student body leader and liaison with administration

Head Boy

Senior male student leader representing boys in the school

Head Girl

Senior female student leader representing girls in the school

Health Prefect

Oversees health-related matters and first aid coordination

Welfare Prefect

Manages student welfare and social support programs

Time Prefect

Ensures punctuality and time management in school activities

Academics Prefect

Coordinates academic activities and study programs

Furniture Prefect

Manages classroom furniture and equipment maintenance

Compound Prefect

Oversees school compound cleanliness and maintenance

Class Prefects

Represent each class and maintain classroom discipline

Sports & Athletics

Football

Our football team has achieved remarkable success in district and regional competitions. Students develop teamwork, discipline, and sportsmanship through regular training and matches.

Athletics

Track and field events including sprints, long distance, jumping, and throwing competitions. Students participate in inter-school athletics meets and district championships.

Volleyball

Both boys and girls volleyball teams compete in school leagues and inter-school tournaments. The sport promotes coordination, strategy, and team spirit.

Basketball

Basketball teams participate in regular training sessions and compete in school tournaments. The sport enhances physical fitness and strategic thinking.

Music, Dance & Drama (MDD)

Music

Students participate in choir, instrumental music, and solo performances. The school choir has won several awards in district and regional competitions.

Dance

Traditional and modern dance performances showcase cultural diversity and artistic expression. Dance groups participate in cultural festivals and competitions.

Drama

The drama club stages plays, skits, and performances addressing social issues, cultural themes, and educational topics. Students develop acting and presentation skills.

Leavers Celebrations

S6 & S4 Leavers Party

Annual celebrations marking the completion of studies for Senior 6 and Senior 4 students. These memorable events include formal ceremonies, entertainment, and farewell activities.

Formal Ceremony

Graduation-style celebration with certificates and awards

Entertainment

Music, dance performances, and cultural presentations

Farewell Activities

Special events and memories shared with classmates

Alumni Network

Connecting graduates with the school community

Study Tours

Educational Tours

Students participate in educational tours to historical sites, museums, industries, and other learning institutions. These tours enhance classroom learning and provide real-world experiences.

Industrial Visits

Visits to factories, farms, and businesses help students understand various career paths and industrial processes. These experiences guide career choices and practical learning.

Inter-House Competitions

House System

Students are organized into houses that compete in various academic, sports, and cultural activities throughout the year. This system promotes healthy competition, teamwork, and school spirit.

Academic Competitions

Quiz competitions, debates, and subject-based challenges

Sports Competitions

Football, athletics, volleyball, and other sports tournaments

Cultural Competitions

Music, dance, drama, and art competitions

Annual Awards

Recognition of outstanding house performance and individual achievements
