Ezra Memorial S.S. Unveils New Science Laboratory

Published: July 10, 2025

Ezra Memorial Secondary School has officially opened its state-of-the-art science laboratory, providing students with modern facilities for hands-on learning and experimentation.

The new lab is equipped with the latest technology and safety features, supporting the school’s commitment to excellence in science education.

“This laboratory will inspire our students to explore, discover, and innovate. It’s a major step forward for our academic programs.”
— Head of Science Department

Students and staff attended the grand opening, expressing excitement for the enhanced learning opportunities the lab will provide.
