Ezra Memorial S.S. Wins Kibuku District Football Tournament!

Published: June 1, 2025

Ezra Memorial Secondary School has made history by clinching its first-ever title in the Kibuku District Football Tournament, sending waves of excitement throughout the school community and beyond.

The tournament brought together top teams from across the district. Ezra Memorial SS displayed unmatched skill, teamwork, and determination, eventually emerging as champions after a thrilling final match, which ended in a victory.

The win marks a major milestone for the school’s sports department and stands as a testament to the talent and discipline of the students.

“This is not just a win in football—it’s a win for the entire Ezra Memorial family. The students have made us proud and shown what dedication can achieve.”
— Head Teacher

Celebrations filled the school compound as students, teachers, and parents gathered to welcome the team back. The football trophy now proudly rests in the school's trophy cabinet, a symbol of hard work, unity, and the beginning of a new chapter in Ezra Memorial's sporting journey.

The school is now setting its sights on regional competitions, hoping to carry forward the same winning spirit.
