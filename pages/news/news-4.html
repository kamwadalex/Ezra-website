Ezra Memorial Celebrates Annual Cultural Festival

Published: September 20, 2025

Ezra Memorial Secondary School recently hosted its annual Cultural Festival, a vibrant celebration of diversity, creativity, and community spirit that brought together students, teachers, parents, and local community members.

The festival featured traditional dances, music performances, art exhibitions, and cultural displays representing various ethnic backgrounds and traditions. Students showcased their talents through poetry recitations, drama performances, and traditional costume parades.

"This festival is not just about entertainment; it's about celebrating our diversity and teaching our students to appreciate different cultures. It's a beautiful way to build unity and understanding."

Highlights of the event included a traditional dance competition, where students performed dances from different regions, and an art exhibition featuring student artwork inspired by cultural themes. The festival also included food stalls offering traditional dishes from various communities.

The event was attended by local dignitaries, community leaders, and families, making it a truly inclusive celebration. The success of this year's festival has inspired plans for an even bigger celebration next year.
